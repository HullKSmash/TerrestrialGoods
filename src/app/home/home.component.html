A plastic-free marketplace

Wondering how you can reduce your consumption of plastic? Care product packaging is a great place to start! By buying products packaged in paper, glass, or metal, you're helping reduce the demand for single-use plastic. If demand is lower, production will be lower, and less plastic will find its way into our environment where it doesn't belong.

At Terrestrial Goods, you can find plastic-free and plastic-minimized products stocked at the retailers you already use. No new stores, no new habits - just trying a new product here and there. We think you'll even fall in love with some of them!
