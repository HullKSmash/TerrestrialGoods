About

Terrestrial Goods is a centralized directory of plastic-free and minimal-plastic personal care products, curated from trusted retailers around the web. We help eco-conscious and planet-aware consumers find brands that are committed to helping our planet by minimizing the use of plastic packaging and containers in care products.

Changing your shopping habits for your care products may seem like a drop in the bucket, but small efforts can add up to a big impact. By choosing products that use more sustainable containers, you contribute to the demand for responsible packaging and demonstrate to manufacturers that it’s a change worth investing in.

A few of our values:

Great care products don’t have to pollute the environment with single-use plastic packaging.

Being good to our planet and its inhabitants is more important than saving pennies on production costs.

Customers deserve quality products produced with care and respect.

Terrestrial Goods may receive a commission on items purchased through links from the site. These proceeds help keep the project running.
